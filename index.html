Our History

EcoThrive Solutions was founded in 2025 by Ava Thompson, a passionate advocate for sustainable living and environmental responsibility. What began as a small initiative to provide eco-friendly school supplies to local classrooms quickly grew into a full-scale sustainable office product company. We started out selling recycled notebooks and compostable pens, and today we offer a wide range of products that serve homes, businesses, and institutions alike.

Over the years, our company has expanded to serve customers nationwide, building strong partnerships with environmentally conscious manufacturers and distributors. Through our continued efforts to reduce waste, support ethical sourcing, and minimize our carbon footprint, we’ve become a trusted name in green business solutions. EcoThrive is more than a brand it's a movement towards a cleaner, more responsible future.

In addition to our commitment to sustainability, we actively participate in local community programs, from donating supplies to underfunded schools to hosting workshops on environmental awareness. As we look to the future, our mission remains clear: to empower people and businesses to thrive without harming the planet.

Why Choose Us

At EcoThrive Solutions, we believe in the power of everyday choices. Our clients choose us because we prioritize quality, sustainability, and social impact. Every product we offer is carefully selected to reduce environmental harm while maintaining high performance and reliability.

We stand out because of our passionate team, transparent policies, and dedication to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, particularly Goals 12 and 13, which focus on responsible consumption and climate action.

Our personalized customer service, flexible policies, and strong ethical foundation make us the preferred choice for clients who care about the future. When you work with EcoThrive, you’re supporting a company that invests in education, local sourcing, and smart solutions for a greener tomorrow.
